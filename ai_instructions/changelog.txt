Victoria 3 Timeline v1.0.1

Bug Fixes
- Fixed duplicate economic boost localization and invalid journal entry fields that could produce startup errors.
- Removed stale auto-build monthly checks that referenced unset variables.
- Updated personal union and dynamic country character checks to use Victoria 3 1.13 ruler and heir triggers.

Production methods
- Deleted the vanilla production method override

AI
- Changed the negative bureaucracy AI boost to trigger at a 5% bureaucracy shortfall instead of a flat -10 deficit.

Treasury/Investment Pool
- Reduced automatic investment pool transfers to keep only 20% of the reserve limit in the treasury and added a 1,000,000 transfer button.

Game Rules & Fixes
- Split AI dynamic country creation into its own Off, Roleplay, and Gameplay rule so AI Improvement can focus on economy automation.
- Fixed dynamic country creation so stale state markers from another country can no longer be absorbed into the wrong new country.
- Made dynamic country expansion use shared GDP-limited clustering instead of separate recognized and unrecognized size weights.
- Reworked dynamic country expansion to grow through ordered GDP-budget passes instead of random pass counts.
- Increased dynamic country expansion breadth for major and great powers while keeping GDP limits in place.
- Made major and great powers always attempt dynamic country expansion instead of occasionally creating one-state subjects.
- Routed dynamic countries created by subject nations to the top overlord so low-autonomy subjects do not lose them to independence.
- Restored normal dynamic country subject creation for tributaries and dominions, which can hold subjects.
- Blocked chartered company subjects from creating dynamic countries.

Culture Merging
- Expanded centralized culture merging to cover Eastern German, Cree, Inuit, and additional South Asian cultures for better late-game performance.
- Added Luri, Champa, Philippine, and southern African culture consolidation to reduce small pop fragmentation while keeping regional targets plausible.
- Added Bashkir, Chuvash, Chechen, and Circassian centralized culture consolidation for cleaner Russia and North Caucasus pop maps.
- Merged Scottish Gaelic into Scottish in centralized culture consolidation to reduce British Isles pop fragmentation.
- Added Mesoamerican native culture consolidation into Nahua for Zapotec, Tarascan, and Maya pops.
- Added South American culture consolidation for Aimara, Guarani, Sulista, and Paulista pops.
- Added Mazanderani, Buryat, Wolof, and southern Nigerian culture consolidation for cleaner regional pop maps.
- Added Haratin and Asturleonese culture consolidation into nearby regional targets.
- Added Assyrian and Mari culture consolidation and removed the beta label from the culture merging game rule.
- Added culture merge cleanup to decentralized countries as well
- Added Mordvin culture consolidation into the Volga-Uralic merge path.
