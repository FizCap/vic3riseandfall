Victoria 3 Timeline v1.0.3

Autobuilder
- Removed treasury reserve limits from the manual Government Administration button while keeping queue and target checks.
- Updated AI Government Administration auto-build logic to count gold reserves plus 20% of the investment pool before queuing new buildings.
- Removed treasury reserve limits from manual University and Power Plant construction while keeping queue, technology, and target checks.
- Reworked University construction to cover every eligible state with one university and stop there.
- Fixed University auto-build so one active university construction no longer blocks coverage in other eligible states.
- Relaxed player Railway, Port, Power Plant, and Construction Sector auto-build checks so one active construction no longer blocks other valid player auto-build targets.
- Added dynamic affordability-based player auto-build batching that scales monthly construction with surplus, reserve health, valid targets, and government queue room.
- Changed University auto-build to coverage-only, stopping once every eligible state has one built or queued University instead of expanding by GDP.
- Updated manual and automatic University coverage to prioritize the highest-GDP eligible states first.
- Reordered autobuilder controls so each manual construction button appears next to its matching auto-build toggle.
- Made autobuilder buttons player-only and moved AI construction automation out of scripted button chance logic into monthly effects.
- Fixed the missing Government Administration construction queue trigger localization.

Dynamic Countries
- Prevented AI subject nations from creating dynamic countries while keeping dynamic country creation available for player subjects.

Personal Unions
- Added a Personal Unions game rule that toggled personal union succession events and yearly age-weighted ruler or heir death rolls for monarchies, with medical technologies reducing the chance.
